Meet Your Instructors

Rob Michaeson

Emerging Screenwriting Talent

Rob Michaelson is an emerging screenwriting talent whose first feature film, Warning, was sold to Lions Gate, released in theaters, and can also be found streaming on AppleTV+ and Amazon Prime. It was hailed by Variety as, "thought-provoking yet unpretentiously presented” and stars Thomas Jane, Alice Eve, Annabelle Wallis and Rupert Everett. He also won best screenplay at the Sochi International Film Festival and best feature at the Trieste Sci_fi Film fest. His latest feature Joshua Tree has been optioned and is looking to be in production by Spring 2022. Rob currently resides in Laurel Canyon, where he enjoys staring into the abyss and cackling at the madness of existence.

Melissa Butler

Screenwriter/Producer

Melissa Butler is a writer/producer who began her screenwriting and film career behind the scenes on several films including Haunting Past, Sunflower Express, VR-20, and In Love with Hip Hop. With a Bachelor's in Film and a Master's in Creative Writing, Melissa specializes in screenplay development and polishing with a specific knack for crafting stories with production in mind. She has also published a series of chapbooks and novellas. Based in North Carolina, she works with clients to bring their concepts to the page and screen. She has had two features optioned, one of which is currently in development. You can connect with her on CLUBHOUSE
